California

Love.

The ultimate unifier. In February 2018, friends and family nationwide converged in beautiful Dana Point, California to bare witness to the marriage of two lovely souls. Ever the opportunist, I took this occasion to produce a short film chronicling the events that transpired during this long weekend in Southern California. Edited with Adobe Premier and shot on a Canon Vixia HF R800, this short film is a peek into the lives of lifelong friends gathering for one of life's most precious moments.
