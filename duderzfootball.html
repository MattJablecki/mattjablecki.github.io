Fantasy Football. The perfect marriage of sports and nerdom. Millions of people annually draft their rosters, spend hours on the waiver wire, and meticulously research which players will give them bragging rights at the water cooler on Monday morning. My friends and I are no different. Founded in 2013, the Duderz Football league was created as a way for my childhood friends, located all across the country, to stay in touch, but more importantly, prove once and for all who is the best Fantasy Football GM. Using Yahoo Sports as our preferred platform, we have had six consecutive successful campaigns, with a seventh rapidly approaching. While Yahoo offers an unbelievable amount of record keeping capabilities, I always felt there was an opportunity for a more customized experience, catered specifically to our league. From this notion, Duderz Football Archive was born.

Utilizing the available statistics on Yahoo, I was able to create a massive relational database customized to study a wide array of stats related to our league. Harnessing SQL, Duderz Football Archive allows you to search individual achievments, such as how many touchdowns you scored in 2014, for example, as well as league wide stats like 'what is my all time record vs. player x?' A labor of love, Duderz Football Archive will be updated annually to allow for the managers in our league to accurately measure their skills on a tremendously in-depth scale.

Still currently in development, Duderz Football Archive will be available in Summer 2019.
