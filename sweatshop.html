Located in the Olneyville district of Providence, RI, 'The Sweatshop' was an underground performance space/skate spot existing from 2008 - 2010. During its brief existence, The Sweatshop established itself as a haven for artists and skateboarders alike, garnering national attention and drawing bands from across the globe. In keeping with the tradition of the underground Olneyville scene, all shows and performances were strictly word of mouth, as the operation of such a space wasn't necessarily 'legal.' In spite of this restriction, or possibly because of it, hundreds of locals would converge at the Sweatshop for a chance to experience a totally unique live music experience unlike any venue you could imagine.

A basic static website, The Sweatshop Retrospective gives the visitor a snapshot of a moment in time where you could experience a truly exciting musical act unfettered by rules and restrictions.
