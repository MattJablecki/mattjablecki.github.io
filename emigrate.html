Emigrate Studios

Founded in Portland, OR 2011, Emigrate Studios was a recording studio co-owned and operated by brothers Matt and Marc Jablecki. Emigrate produced and hosted a wildly diverse group of artists during its seven year existence, closing its doors in 2018.
