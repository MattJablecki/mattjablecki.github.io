Emigrate Studios

Founded in Portland, OR 2011, Emigrate Studios was a recording studio co-owned and operated by brothers Matt and Marc Jablecki. Emigrate produced and hosted a wildly diverse group of artists during its seven year existence, closing its doors in 2018. Presented here are just a handful of some of the projects Emigrate had a hand in. Give them a listen.

  • A power trio pumping out Psychedelic Rock, Corduroy visited Emigrate Studios in 2011 to record their debut single "Morning Session."

  • Shy Girls is the nom de plume for L.A. based producer Dan Vidmar. Shy Girls recorded the vocals for his 2013 EP 'Timeshare' at Emigrate Studios.

  • Surf-Punk dynamic duo Funeral Gold write big riffs with even bigger hooks. The boys recorded their EP 'Everything Hurts' at Emigrate back in 2016.

  • Consisting of some of the first songs the band ever played together, Fanno Creek got together with Emigrate in 2012 to record 'End is End'. Well written and catchy as can be, lucky for us this collection of throwback jams was finally put to wax.

  • GRAMMIES is a mind-altering experience, proven here on their 2013 EP 'Award Winning." Recorded at Emigrate and engineered by Dan Vidmar (Shy Girls), this experimental jazz duo take you on a musical journey you won't soon forget.
