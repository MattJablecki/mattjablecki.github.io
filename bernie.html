Portland For Bernie

Fundraiser Compliation

Election 2016.

Without meandering too far into the sticky web that is politics, suffice it to say 2016 was a simpler time. As candidates on both sides of the aisle were gearing up for a Presidential election year, one candidate stood out to me as identifying many of my personal ideals and beliefs. Bernie Sanders, the Independent Democratic Socialist Senator from Vermont, was speaking a language that many people of my generation understood.

Relying heavily on small donations averaging $27, the Sanders campaign was exciting a grassroots movement that inspired many to get active in the democratic process. In Boston, MA, my dear friend and owner of Big Nice Studio, Bradford Kreiger, had the idea to curate a compilation, available on Bandcamp for a donation of your choosing, showcasing local Boston artists who were 'feeling the Bern.' With his blessing, I expanded on this concept and rallied local Portland artists to do the same.

Attracting the attention of the local media, Portland for Bernie was able to raise hundreds of dollars for the Bernie Sanders campaign, as well as provide a platform for local artists to garner some press. While no longer actively fundraising, Portland for Bernie is still available to enjoy. Close your eyes, listen to the tunes, and imagine what could've been.
