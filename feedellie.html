Feed Ellie

In Development

Parenthood presents a number of unforeseen challenges that only make themselves apparent once you take your newborn home and start raising this new bundle of joy. My wife and I experienced one of these challenges firsthand, when our daughter Elliott (Ellie for short), started bottle feeding. Struggling to maintain even a semblance of sanity during bouts of sleep deprivation, one of us would regularly ask the other, 'When was the last time Ellie ate?' While it was easy to generate a guesstimate, I had one of those moments where you look up to the stars and exclaim: 'There has to be a better way!' Introducing: 'Feed Ellie'

Using a timestamp click event, we were able to accurately track when the last time Ellie ate by simply clicking a button on my newly created app. Additionally, Feed Ellie tracks the last time the button was clicked. When Ellie is well fed, her expression is one of contentment. If more than two hours pass, Ellie's expression turns neutral. If you dare allow the time to exceed three hours, digital Ellie begins to get extremelly ornery, satisfied only by a click of the button indicating a feeding. This became a fun and interactive way for my wife and I as new parents to accurately track Ellie's eating patterns.

This app is currently in development.
